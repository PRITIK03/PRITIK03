# 💫 About Me:
I’m an AI Engineer and backend-focused developer with hands-on experience building real-world machine learning and LLM-powered systems. My work sits at the intersection of AI, scalable backend architecture, and practical business applications—where models don’t just exist, but actually solve problems.<br><br>I’ve developed and deployed end-to-end AI solutions using Python, Flask, LangChain, and FAISS, focusing on areas like document intelligence, recommendation systems, and computer vision. From building a Smart Document Analyzer that processes large-scale PDFs with vector search and LLM pipelines, to creating AI-driven systems like RemedyAI and solar panel dust detection models, I enjoy turning complex ideas into production-ready systems.<br><br>On the backend side, I design REST APIs, manage databases, and build scalable workflows that support real-time inference and data processing. My experience includes working with AWS services, integrating APIs, and structuring systems for reliability and maintainability.<br><br>I’ve also contributed to research with an IEEE publication and hold a utility patent in AI-based system design, reflecting my interest in building not just applications—but innovative solutions with real impact.<br><br>
